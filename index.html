Would you like to

Have a perfect musical memory?
Know every score and recording by heart?
Have read every book about music?
Be able to make sense of all that?

We sure would

That's why we at Peachnote are planning a project that will let you,
us and everybody else feel a bit like we could be all that.

This is how it will work

we analyze every score, recording and book that we can get our hands on, find interesting connections between them and make these connections accessible through easy to use interfaces, so that your musical knowledge stretches further and further, click for click

Some examples of what you'll be able to do

  • Hear how each place in a score sounds in various performances
  • See how composers influenced each other throughout the history
  • See how musical pieces are connected by quotations throughout the history
  • See the differences between various sheet music editions
  • See the differences between multiple interpretations
  • See how similar or dissimilar composers and musicians are based on how their style
